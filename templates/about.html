Overview

StatShark provides insights on the historical performance of nfl wagers. Users can query over 40 years of data to maximize their return on investment. Usage examples are provided below:

Query Team Performance Against Spread

StatShark allows users to query the performance of a given team based on various criteria. In the example below, We wish to test the theory that the Dallas Cowboys, due to their status as 'America's team', has performed poorly against the spread. As a first step, we query the performance of the Dallas Cowboys against the spread for the last 10 years. We enter our search criteria as so:

When we click on the Submit button, we are first shown a high level summary or our results:



This does not seem to confirm our suspicions, as the performance is basically par. We can click on Game data to see a breakout of the results. As we scroll through the table, we realize that their performance against the spread was quite good in the early years of our data set, but that it dropped significantly later on. This makes us question whether 2004 is an appropriate starting point. It may make more sense to limit our query to the Tony Romo era. Specifically, we want to know how the team performs agains the spread in high profile games, where we suspect the line may be influenced by a large amount of casual bettors.
